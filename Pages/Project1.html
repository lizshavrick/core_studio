The Green Budget

Liz Shavrick

Research question

How can we combat the inaffordability of sustainability for the average consumer?

I am fascinated in this question because sustainability amongst individuals is crucial and adds up to make a key difference. However, it is sumply too expensive to live completely sustainable lives when living on a budget. That is why I propose The Green Budget: an app that helps you budget your money to live more sustainably.

User survey

Ideation


The main challenges discovered from my survey were that people are unaware, lazy, or can't afford to be sustainable. Although this original poster idea wasn't pursued, it helped me realize my main focus of this project would be affordability, and led me to my actual idea.

My idea is to create a budgeting app not to make sustainability more affordable, but to help people find a way to afford sustainability.
